Privacy by Design

At Svashasan, we believe data privacy is as critical as vehicle safety. You have full control over what information you share and how your data is handled.

Local by Default

Dashcam and Sentry Mode footage is stored on your vehicle's local storage and is never transmitted to us unless you explicitly choose to share it.

End-to-End Encryption

All communication between your Svashasan app and the vehicle uses industry-leading encryption to prevent unauthorized access.

You Own Your Data

You can request a copy of your personal data or ask for the deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Data Management Request

Submit a request to access, export, or delete your Svashasan data.

Your request has been submitted successfully. Our privacy team will review and respond within 30 days.

How we handle Fleet Learning data

To improve Autopilot and Full Self-Driving capabilities, Svashasan uses "fleet learning." Short video clips and sensor data may be uploaded to our servers to train neural networks. This data is fully anonymized and is not linked to your VIN or account unless a critical safety event is detected. You can opt-out of sharing this data at any time via your vehicle's touchscreen settings.

Sentry Mode and Dashcam Privacy

Camera footage is processed in real-time on your vehicle. We do not have access to your Dashcam or Sentry Mode recordings. These are stored on a USB drive plugged into the vehicle's glovebox or center console. The only exception is if you choose to "Share with Svashasan" specifically after a safety incident to help us understand model behavior.

Privacy Policy

Date of Effectiveness: July 27, 2025
Last Updated: July 27, 2025

We at स्वशासन are dedicated to protecting and honoring the privacy of each and every person who uses our services and visits our website. This Privacy Policy describes your rights and options regarding your data, as well as how we gather, use, store, and safeguard personal information. You agree to the following practices by using our website going forward.

Information We Collect

Your name, email address, and phone number are just a few examples of the personal information we might gather when you use our website. This typically occurs when you submit a form to get in touch with us, ask for updates, express interest in joining our waitlist, or offer feedback.

Additionally, we automatically collect limited technical data, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing habits, using cookies or comparable technologies. This facilitates user experience improvement and traffic analysis.

How We Use Your Information

This information is only collected and used to operate and improve our services. We may use your information to respond to your inquiries, provide you with details about our EV OS product, examine usage trends, and enhance the functionality of our website. We may occasionally send you emails with announcements, product updates, or early access opportunities if you consent. Opting out of such communications will always be possible.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We never sell your personal information to third parties. We put in place appropriate organizational, technical, and administrative safeguards against misuse, disclosure, and illegal access. No online transmission method is completely risk-free, even though we work hard to guarantee the highest levels of data security.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, update, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) of India, and other applicable frameworks.

Links to Third-Party Websites

Links to external websites or services may be found on our website. We urge users to check those external websites on their own as we have no control over their content or privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

We don't intentionally gather data from kids younger than thirteen. We will act quickly to remove such data if we learn that it was gathered without parental consent.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect modifications to our procedures, regulatory requirements, or service offerings. This page will be updated with the new date as soon as we do. Your continued use of our website following such modifications indicates that you agree to the updated policy.

Contact Us

Please send an email to contact@svashasan.shop with any queries, requests, or clarifications pertaining to your personal information. We pledge to promptly and politely address any privacy concerns.

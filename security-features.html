Built for Your Peace of Mind

Svashasan is engineered to be the most secure vehicle on the road. From digital encryption to physical intrusion detection, your safety is our ultimate priority.

REC: SENTRY MODE ACTIVE CAM: REAR VIEW
Always Watching

Guardian Mode

When parked, Svashasan uses its external cameras to monitor its surroundings 24/7. If a potential threat is detected, the car activates its alarm, records the event, and sends an instant alert to your mobile device.

  • 360° Vision: Multiple vantage points to capture every angle.
  • Dashcam: Automatic recording of all driving incidents.

PIN to Drive

Prevent unauthorized use with an additional layer of security. Enter a unique 4-digit code on the touchscreen before your vehicle can be shifted out of park.

End-to-End Encryption

Your data belongs to you. All communication between your vehicle, the Svashasan App, and our servers is secured with industry-leading AES-256 encryption.

Secure Mobile Access

Unlock, precondition, and start your vehicle from anywhere. Our digital key uses proximity-based Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) with rolling codes to prevent relay attacks.

Privacy by Design

Our cabin cameras are disabled by default for all private vehicles. When enabled (for Autopilot monitoring), data is processed locally on the car's hardware and is never stored or transmitted unless you choose to share it with us to help improve safety for everyone.

Certified GDPR & Data Privacy Compliant

Always Up to Date

Cybersecurity threats evolve, and so does Svashasan. Our security team continuously monitors for vulnerabilities and deploys over-the-air security patches instantly, keeping your vehicle protected against the latest threats.

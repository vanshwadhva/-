Recall Essentials

Are recalls free?

Yes. All safety-related recall repairs are completed free of charge to the vehicle owner. This includes both physical hardware repairs and software-based solutions.

How do Over-the-Air (OTA) updates work?

Many recalls for Svashasan vehicles are resolved through software updates. Your vehicle will download the update via Wi-Fi or Cellular and prompt you to install it. No visit to a service center is required for these fixes.

How will I be notified?

If your vehicle is affected by a recall, Svashasan will notify you via the mobile app, in-car touchscreen, and through a formal letter sent to your registered mailing address.

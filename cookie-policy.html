By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as outlined in this policy, unless you choose to disable them through your browser settings.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. These files store certain data that help websites recognize your device and remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language or the pages you viewed. Cookies are commonly used to improve functionality, security, and performance, as well as to provide insights that help us enhance our services.

How We Use Cookies

We may use different types of cookies for various purposes. Essential cookies are required for the basic functionality of the website and cannot be disabled. These include cookies that allow you to navigate the site, submit forms, or access secure areas. Performance cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the site by collecting anonymous usage statistics. Functional cookies remember your preferences and choices to offer a more personalized experience. Lastly, in the future, we may use analytical or third-party cookies to gain deeper insights or improve integrations—for example, with tools like Google Analytics or social media plugins.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. However, you can control and manage cookies directly through your browser settings. You may choose to block or delete cookies at any time. Please note that if you disable certain types of cookies, some features of the website may not function as intended or may be unavailable.

To manage cookies in your browser, refer to the help documentation provided by the browser you use (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Additionally, third-party analytics providers like Google offer their own opt-out mechanisms if you do not wish your data to be tracked for analytical purposes.

Data Privacy

We do not use cookies to collect sensitive personal information, nor do we sell or share cookie-related data with third parties for advertising or profiling. Any data collected through cookies is handled in accordance with our broader Privacy Policy, and is used solely for improving your experience with our services.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or our own data practices. When we make changes, we will revise the “Last Updated” date above. We recommend that you review this policy periodically to stay informed.

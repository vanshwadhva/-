Modular Architecture.

Build your ultimate autonomy stack. Svashasan OS is designed with a highly decoupled architecture, allowing OEMs to integrate specific software modules that perfectly align with their hardware capabilities and product roadmaps.

Core Perception

Required Base Module

The mandatory foundational brain. It processes raw data from cameras, radars, and optional LiDAR to create a real-time 3D bounding box map of the vehicle's environment.

  • 360° Object & Lane Detection
  • Sensor Fusion (Kalman/EKF)
  • Traffic Sign & Light Recognition

Highway Pilot (L2++)

Included in Modular FSD

Designed for high-speed expressways. Enables hands-off, eyes-on supervised driving with intelligent speed adaptation and automated passing logic.

  • Automated Lane Change (ALC)
  • Overtake & Merge Assist
  • Predictive Curve Assist

Urban Pilot (L3)

Exclusive to Elite Stack

Handles complex city scenarios. Requires robust map data and advanced sensor suites to navigate chaotic urban environments safely.

  • Intersection Management
  • Roundabout Navigation
  • Traffic Jam Chauffeur (<60km /h)

Active Safety Suite

Required Base Module

A critical module focusing on accident prevention. It overrides user control in emergencies to brake or steer away from imminent collisions.

  • Multi-scenario AEB
  • Blind Spot Intervention
  • Cross-Traffic Braking

DMS & Cabin

Plug-and-play Module

Monitors the vehicle's interior to ensure driver readiness, a regulatory requirement for advanced supervised autonomy (L2+) in many regions.

  • Drowsiness & Yawn Detection
  • Cognitive Load & Distraction
  • Occupant Sensing

Advanced Parking

Plug-and-play Module

Takes the stress out of low-speed maneuvering. From basic guidance lines to fully remote-controlled parking via mobile app.

  • 360° Stitched Surround View
  • Automated Parallel/Perpendicular
  • App-based Remote Parking

Seamless Integration.

Svashasan OS isn't just a collection of features; it's rigorously engineered middleware that slides neatly into standard automotive architectures.

AUTOSAR Adaptive Ready

Built to interface effortlessly with standard AUTOSAR environments and Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS), ensuring strict execution timing and memory isolation.

OTA Update Pipeline

Secure, encrypted Over-The-Air infrastructure. Update perception models, HD maps, and driving policies on the fly, complete with rollback protection and integrity validation.

ASIL-D Compliance

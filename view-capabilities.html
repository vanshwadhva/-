Capability Suite

Explore the technical maneuvers and intelligent decision-making that define the Svashasan Pilot experience.

City Street Driving

Navigate complex urban environments with ease. The system identifies lane markings, curbs, and traffic flow to steer through tight corners and busy intersections.

  • • Right and left-hand turns
  • • Yielding to oncoming traffic
  • • Roundabout navigation

Vulnerable Road User Detection

Our vision-first approach provides 360-degree awareness of pedestrians, cyclists, and animals, predicting their movements to ensure safety.

  • • Real-time distance estimation
  • • Prediction of movement trajectories
  • • Low-light vision enhancement

Traffic Light & Sign Control

Seamlessly handles stops at red lights and stop signs. The vehicle waits for the light to turn green and confirms the path is clear before proceeding.

  • • Recognition of flashing signals
  • • Multi-way stop sign priority logic
  • • Temporary construction signage detection

Highway Navigation

Full-stack highway capability including merge assistance, lane centering, and high-speed cruise control that adapts to traffic density.

  • • Adaptive cruise control (0-150 km/h)
  • • Automatic overtaking of slow vehicles
  • • Precision lane centering

Active Safety Features

Included standard with every Svashasan Pilot suite.

Emergency Braking

Automatic application of brakes if a collision is imminent.

Blind Spot Monitoring

Visual and audible alerts when vehicles enter adjacent lanes.

Obstacle Awareness

Limits acceleration if an obstacle is detected in the travel path.

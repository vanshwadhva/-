New Owner Guide

Welcome to Svashasan

Get to know your vehicle and the Svashasan Operating System. Follow these steps to set up your profile, connect your app, and experience the future of autonomous driving.

1

Account Setup

Your Svashasan Account is the command center for your vehicle. Before taking delivery or starting your first drive, ensure your profile is complete.

  • Download the Svashasan App
  • Add Payment Method for Charging
  • Upload Driver's License Documents
Go to Account Settings →
2

Keys & Access

Your phone is your primary key. Using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), your vehicle detects you as you approach and automatically unlocks.

Setting up Phone Key

Open the App > Controls > Locks > Set Up Phone Key. Ensure Bluetooth is always enabled.

Note: Always carry your backup NFC Card provided in the glovebox.

3

Autopilot & Safety

Svashasan OS is powered by our advanced Supervised Learning models. Before engaging Autopilot, familiarize yourself with the limitations.

  • Calibrate cameras by driving 20-50km on marked roads.
  • Double pull the gear stalk to engage Traffic-Aware Cruise Control.
  • Keep hands on the wheel at all times.

Auto Lane Change

While driving on the highway, Svashasan Pilot will position your vehicle in the optimal lane to prepare for merges and exits while overtaking slow-moving traffic.

Convenience

Arrive at your destination and let the car do the rest. Auto-Dock identifies parallel and perpendicular parking spots and maneuvers into them with millimeter precision. With Smart Summon, your car will navigate complex parking lots to come find you.

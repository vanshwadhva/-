Always On Guard

Sentry Mode

When parked, Sentry Mode continuously monitors the environment. If it detects a suspicious movement or impact, it automatically enters an 'Alert' state, recording footage and notifying you instantly via the Svashasan App.

Proximity Detection

Intelligently identifies potential threats while ignoring harmless bystanders.

Live Camera View

Stream live video from your car's cameras directly to your phone from anywhere.

On-Road Witness

Built-in Dashcam

Every drive is automatically recorded. Dashcam continuously captures footage of your surroundings while driving, ensuring you have a digital record of any incident or beautiful scenery you encounter.

  • Auto-Record on Impact
  • One-Tap Manual Clip Save
  • Honk-to-Save Feature

Privacy First Storage

We believe your data belongs to you. All Dashcam and Sentry Mode footage is processed locally on your vehicle's custom silicon and stored on a physical USB drive. No video is ever sent to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Encrypted Footage

Recorded clips are encrypted on-disk, requiring your unique vehicle PIN to access or export from the touchscreen.

No Subscription Required

Unlike competitors, 360° security recording is a standard feature on every Svashasan. No monthly fees for your own security.

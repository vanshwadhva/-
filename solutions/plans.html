Which OS is Right for Your Fleet?

Svashasan OS is designed to scale across your entire vehicle lineup, from affordable mass-market entries to flagship autonomy platforms.

Svashasan OS Basic

What it offers

A highly stable, production-grade ADAS suite focusing on SAE L1/L2 essentials. It packages the most commercially deployable safety functions (AEB, Lane Keeping, Adaptive Cruise) into a clean, OEM-friendly offering optimized for regulatory readiness.

Who is it for?

Mass-market vehicle models, entry-level EVs, and commercial fleets where cost-efficiency and absolute baseline safety are the top priorities.

Best Audience

OEMs looking to achieve 5-star NCAP ratings and comply with international safety standards (like GSR2 in Europe) without the overhead of complex, full self-driving hardware.

Most Popular

Svashasan OS Modular FSD

What it offers

The ultimate bridge to supervised autonomy. It introduces L2++ Highway Pilot for hands-off driving on controlled roads, automated lane changes, comprehensive parking solutions, and an advanced Driver Monitoring System (DMS) to ensure driver readiness.

Who is it for?

Mid-to-high-end consumer vehicles aiming to provide a premium "supervised self-driving" experience on highways and long commutes.

Best Audience

Commuters who spend heavy hours on the highway and desire significant fatigue reduction. It allows OEMs to compete directly with advanced supervised systems currently dominating the EV market.

Svashasan OS Elite

What it offers

The flagship, no-compromises autonomy stack. It introduces true SAE Level 3 capabilities, including a fully hands-off/eyes-off Traffic Jam Chauffeur, complex urban intersection management, LiDAR integration, and full ASIL-D fail-safe redundancy.

Who is it for?

Flagship luxury vehicles, cutting-edge technology demonstrators, and advanced mobility fleets preparing for the transition to robotaxis.

Best Audience

Premium OEMs catering to early tech adopters who demand the absolute pinnacle of autonomous capabilities, where the car confidently takes over full driving responsibilities in designated operational design domains (ODD).
